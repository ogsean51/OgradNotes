### Kinematics
---
$$
\begin{align}
V = \frac{Vf + Vi}{2} \\ \\

V = \frac{\Delta x}{t} \\ \\

Vf^{2}= Vi^{2}+ 2 \cdot a \cdot t^2 \\ \\

\Delta x = Vi \cdot t + \frac{1}{2} \cdot a \cdot t^2 \\ \\

a = \frac{Vf - Vi}{t}
\end{align}
$$
### Forces
---
$$
\begin{align}
\Sigma F = m \cdot a \\\\
Fg = m \cdot g \\ \\

Ff = \mu \cdot Fn \\ \\

Fs = k \cdot x
\end{align}
$$

$\mu$ = Friction coefficient
