___
- In free fall (no air resistance)
- Velocity will have 2 components
	- Horizontal
	- Vertical
![[JP - ProjectilesEmbed1|1000]]

### Components
___
- Components are independent
- Split (almost) all kinematic quantities into x and y components

![[JP - ProjectilesEmbed2|1000]]

### Acceleration
___
- Vertical component is just acceleration in free fall (-9.8m/s<sup>2</sup>)
- Horizontal component is ZERO
	- Constant speed horizontally
